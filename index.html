Instructions

For play with BricksBreakerJS follow the instructions below:

  • To move the paddle, you can use the left and right arrow keys or move the mouse over the scenario.
  • To start, click on any surface of the scenario or press the UP arrow key.
  • You have only 3 lives to try to break all bricks to pass to the next level and finish the game.
  • If the ball touch the bottom of the scenario, you will lose a life.
